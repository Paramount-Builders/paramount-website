Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer 24/7 emergency restoration services?

Yes. We respond to emergency calls within minutes and can typically be on-site within 1-2 hours in the greater Hartford area. For the rest of Connecticut, our response time is typically 2-4 hours. Call (860) 505-9335 any time — we're available 24/7/365.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Absolutely. We work directly with all major insurance carriers including The Hartford, Travelers, State Farm, Allstate, Liberty Mutual, USAA, and more. We handle all documentation, communicate directly with your adjuster, and can bill your insurance company directly so you don't have to deal with the paperwork.

How quickly can you respond to water damage?

Every minute counts with water damage. We dispatch teams immediately upon receiving your call. Our goal is on-site within 1-2 hours in the Hartford metro area. We begin water extraction, set up drying equipment, and start preventing secondary damage (mold) right away.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Connecticut. Our main base is in Windsor (Hartford County), and we regularly work throughout Hartford County, New Haven County, Fairfield County, Middlesex County, and beyond. No job is too far — if you're in CT, we'll be there.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Paramount Builders LLC is a fully licensed and insured Connecticut contractor with general liability and workers' compensation coverage. Our technicians hold IICRC certifications in water restoration, fire restoration, and mold remediation. We are also an EPA Lead-Safe Certified Firm.

How much does water damage restoration cost?

Cost varies based on the extent of damage, size of the affected area, and type of water (clean, gray, or black). Most homeowner insurance policies cover water damage restoration. We provide free on-site assessments and work directly with your insurance to maximize your claim. Call us for a free estimate.
